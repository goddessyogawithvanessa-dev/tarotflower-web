Stay true to the seeds of dreams you planted at the new moon, even if obstacles or resistance begin to appear on your path today. The first quarter moon always brings a crisis point where we need to support our manifestations with consistent action, so keep that in mind today! These are the growing pains of blossoming dreams!

This moon is in [Scorpio](https://tarotflower.com/astrology/scorpio-dates-traits-the-phoenix-rising/), which incites us to probe into emotions deeply. As you get to the bottom of things you might feel a bit overwhelmed by the number of options before you. [The Seven of Cups](https://tarotflower.com/tarot-card-meanings/seven-of-cups-tarot/) suggests that you might be wondering which of your options are real and which are illusions.

This might lead you feel like there is a conflict between what you want and what you need, but if you stay true to your heart, your intuition will guide you well. The Star encourages you to remain hopeful! Your dreams will take time to grow, so use any obstacles you encounter to fuel your dedication. Don't get distracted! Stay focused on the actions that support your manifestation just like [The Eight of Pentacles](https://tarotflower.com/tarot-card-meanings/eight-of-pentacles-tarot/). With patience and dedication, your dreams will soon bloom!
