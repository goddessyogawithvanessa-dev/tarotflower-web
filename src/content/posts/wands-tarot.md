      <h2>The Element of Fire, Our Passions</h2>
      <p>The Suit of Wands in the Tarot represents the element of fire, which is symbolic of passion, creativity, and energy. This suit is associated with the zodiac signs Aries, Leo, and Sagittarius, which are all known for their fiery nature. Wands also represent action and movement, and they can indicate a time of taking action and making things happen. When this suit appears in a Tarot reading, it can suggest a need for more energy and drive in your life.</p>
      <p>In terms of its symbolism, the Wand is a phallic symbol representing masculine energy and the power to create. It's a symbol of growth, power, and passion. Wands can also represent intuition and psychic abilities, as they relate to the element of fire and the energy that comes with it. This suit can indicate a time of growth and expansion in your life, both spiritually and creatively.</p>
    </div>

  </section>

  <section class="minor-suit-feature">
    <div class="minor-copy">
      <p>When the Suit of Wands appears in a Tarot reading, it often relates to our passions and creative pursuits. It can indicate that we need to focus on our inner fire and use our creativity to bring new ideas and projects to life. This suit can also suggest that we need to take risks and be bold in pursuing our dreams. It's a sign that we need to harness our inner power and move forward with confidence.</p>
    </div>
    <img src="/images/2023/04/tf-ai-rods-980x980.jpg" alt="Wands tarot artwork by Vanessa Hylande" loading="lazy" />
  </section>

  <section class="minor-suit-feature">
    <div class="minor-copy">
      <p>Overall, the Suit of Wands in the Tarot is a powerful symbol of energy, creativity, and passion. It can indicate a time of growth, action, and new beginnings. When this suit appears in a Tarot reading, it's important to pay attention to its message and use its energy to move forward in your life. Whether you're starting a new project, pursuing a creative passion, or simply seeking to tap into your inner power, the Suit of Wands can be a powerful ally on your journey.</p>
