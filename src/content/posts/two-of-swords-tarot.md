Are you unable to make up your mind? Are you faced with a difficult decision? The Two of Swords Tarot card is one of indecision and duality. It is a card of inner conflict and struggle, and symbolizes that there are two opposing forces at work.

The 2 of swords tarot card can represent a stalemate in a situation. Sometimes we feel frozen, unable to see the full picture, sitting in limbo, unable to see the big picture. Do you go right or left? Do you choose yes or no?

The advice of the Two of Swords tarot card is always the same: It's time to make a choice. Use your logic and your intuition, and consider all options from a place of stillness and quiet.

## The 2 of Swords in your Tarot card reading

The Two of Swords Tarot card encourages you to trust your instincts and tap into your inner knowing. It reminds you that sometimes the answers you seek are not found in the external world, but within yourself. By accessing your intuition and embracing a calm and balanced mindset, you can navigate through the fog of uncertainty and make a decision that aligns with your highest good.

The Two of Swords also highlights the importance of communication and seeking different perspectives. Consider seeking advice or guidance from trusted individuals who can provide valuable insights and help you gain a clearer understanding of the situation.

## 2 of Swords in the Rider Waite

![two of swords tarot card meaning rider waite](https://tarotflower.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/14.jpg)

In the traditional [Rider-Waite tarot deck](https://amzn.to/42AXJz7), the imagery of the Two of Swords card is of two swords crossed in the middle, with a blindfolded figure in the center. The blindfold symbolizes that the querent is unable to see the full picture, and therefore must rely on their intuition and inner guidance to make the best decision. The crossed swords symbolize the opposing forces at work, and the struggle between them.

## Two of Swords love

In a love reading, the Two of Swords tarot card suggests that you may be facing a period of indecision or conflicting emotions in your romantic relationship. It signifies a need to pause and reflect on your feelings and the choices you have regarding the relationship. It could indicate a state of emotional detachment or a fear of making a wrong decision.

In a love tarot reading, the 2 of swords advises you to examine your own needs and desires, as well as those of your partner, to gain clarity and understanding. It encourages open and honest communication, as well as a willingness to explore different perspectives. The Two of Swords reminds you to trust your intuition and seek a balance between your heart and mind before making any significant relationship decisions.

## Embody the Tarot: The lessons of the 2 of swords

![heart chakra mandala embody the tarot midjourney art vanessa hylande](https://tarotflower.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/12/heart-chakra-mandala.jpg)

The Two of Swords is a reminder that difficult choices are sometimes necessary, and it is important to move forward with them in order to find balance. It is a call to make decisions based on logic and reason, rather than just emotion. In the end, the querent must be willing to make a difficult decision in order to find the best outcome. This is how you can embody the tarot - the lessons of the 2 of swords:

1. Create a safe space for yourself
2. Write a list of the pros and cons of each possible choice you face
3. Engage in a [heart chakra opening meditation](https://goddess-yoga-retreat.com/the-best-heart-chakra-yoga-poses-activating-opening-and-balancing-your-anahata-chakra/) to connect with your true desires
4. Channel the divine feminine to see which option, given the pros and cons, resonates most deeply with you: What *feels* right?
