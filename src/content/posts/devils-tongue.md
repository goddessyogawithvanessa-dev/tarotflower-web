Devil’s tongue lashes out at me  
Do I stay or do I run to be free?  
Grip of steel chokes the air from me  
Does love love even this?  
Is love strong enough to endure this?

I am afraid to put my child through this  
Is there a place where it is truly safe to exist?  
I do not feel I should be asking you this  
You paint a cruel picture with your words

You cannot control which way I turn  
How many bridges must we burn  
Before we find ourselves safe within each other  
Will we be stranded there  
An island with no others?

Devil’s tongue, stay away from me  
Injured pride go back home to sleep  
Hurtful cries do not strike at me  
I find it hard to love this  
Doubts are harder to vanquish

Devil’s tongue lose your way with speech  
Sympathy you will not have from me  
Cutting out my eyes so that you can see  
I will not pay for your lost pride  
By crawling on my knees at your side

Devil’s tongue lashes out at me  
Do I stay or do I run to be free?  
Grip of steel chokes the air from me  
Does love love even this?
