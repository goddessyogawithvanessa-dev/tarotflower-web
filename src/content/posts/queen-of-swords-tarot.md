The Queen of Swords tarot card is associated with the element of Air and is affiliated with the astrological signs of Libra and Aquarius. This card is associated with logic, objectivity, and truth. She has a sharp wit and an unwavering sense of justice. The Queen of Swords is a card of cutting through the lies and seeing the truth in any situation.

This minor arcana court card is a formidable figure. She is a powerful woman who uses her intellect and sharp tongue to get what she wants and she is not afraid to challenge convention and speak her truth. The Queen of Swords is direct and to the point, never mincing words. She is a leader, someone who can be trusted to make the right decisions and stand up for what she believes in.

The Queen of Swords is a card of detachment and objectivity. She is able to look at any situation with clarity and make decisions that are logical and fair. Her keen insight allows her to read other people’s true intentions and motivations. She is not swayed by emotion or drama, but rather looks at facts and data in order to make the best decision.

## The Queen of Swords Advice

The Queen of Swords can be a sign that it is time to take a step back and look at your situation objectively. Be honest with yourself and others and to speak your truth. You may need to be more assertive than usual and stand up for what you believe in.

This card can also be a sign to trust your intuition and to be more discerning about who you let into your life. The Queen of Swords is a powerful card that can help you make the best decisions and stand up for what you know to be right. She is a reminder to use your intellect and intuition to make wise decisions and be true to yourself.
