Venus emerges from her shadow today and The Sun, [The Magician](/the-magician-tarot), and [The Three of Pentacles](/three-of-pentacles-tarot) herald the beginning of something new and beautiful in your life. This is a powerful trio of opportunity. [The Ten of Cups](/ten-of-cups-tarot) sits at the bottom of the deck foretelling a joyful ending. When we release darkness, the beautiful light shines in. Though, we must be courageous and tenacious enough to see it through.

Although this week may still be wrought with intense and complicated energies due to the current astrological movements, remember that change always brings opportunity. Be brave and bold as you embody everything that you love and deserve. The Sun is smiling upon you as you manifest your dreams. Use this trio of opportunity to manifest your desires.
