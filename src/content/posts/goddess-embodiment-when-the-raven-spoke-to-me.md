One morning, in that lucid dream state somewhere between dreaming and wakefulness, I heard a fierce tapping on my window. My body couldn’t move and my eyes weren’t open, but somehow I could see my room where I lay.

Suddenly, a raven came through my window and flew at my head. I could feel it fiercely fluttering around my face but I was paralyzed, I couldn’t swat it away.

Before I knew what was happening, the raven flew *inside* my head and in an instant, I found myself in a trance state - I was journeying through time and space.

As I opened to my dream state, I saw a magnificent eagle covered in feathers and jewels perched before me. This powerful creature looked me in the eye with the deepest intensity. In front of us, a grand landscape with red mountains and canyons stretched as far as the eye could see. I stared in shock and awe as golden, shining hieroglyphs began to scroll across the bright blue sky. It was as if some kind of code were downloading into my mind.

![the great eagle, the Hierophant tarot card meaning, the shaman, scorpio zodiac, spirit animal, Midjourney art by Vanessa Hylande](https://tarotflower.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/hierophant-1.jpg)

And then, in an instant, I was flying. I had shapeshifted. I had downloaded something from the Akasha and the transformation had begun. The greatest challenge of my life was before me, and it would require a complete self-transformation. A total phoenix rising moment.

It was only a few years later that I put all of the pieces together. That eagle was me. The raven had come to me as a grave warning of what was about to happen. In hindsight, the omen was a blessing, but it didn’t make the terror any easier to survive.

Shortly thereafter, my four beautiful sons were taken from me in Playa del Carmen, Mexico.  I went through the darkest places, suddenly alone. At the same time, the world went into lockdown and I was trapped only with the company of my grief and loss.

At first I didn’t know if I wanted to go on with my life. But in that most horrific moment, when the world seemed to be crashing and burning down around me, the Goddess Ixchel spoke to me. And it was her inspiration, guidance and love that pulled me back into myself and reminded me how beautiful life can be. She told me exactly what I needed to do, I just had to learn to listen.

![Goddess Ixchel Midjourney art by Vanessa Hylande](https://tarotflower.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/ixchel.jpg)

Ixchel reminded me that dancing and music is a path back to joy. She showed me how my inner shadows of self-abandonment and self-betrayal were reflecting the same back to me in the form of other people in the 3D world. She spoke to me through song, poetry and channeled visions, and guided me through my self-healing journey.

Now, I want to share what I’ve learned with you, so that you can self-heal too.

I created the Goddess Yoga and Embodiment methods to heal myself, and have been sharing it at beautiful women's retreats around the world to help others heal too. Together, we read **tarot cards**, dive deep into **shadow work**, join together in **moon rituals for manifestation**, support each other on our healing journey, and of course celebrate our bodies with **yoga, dance and music**.

## About Goddess Embodiment Practices

![twin flame separation, divine feminine healing, twin flame sex, twin flame union](https://tarotflower.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/twin-flame-separation-2.jpg)

Goddess embodiment practices can offer a range of benefits for individuals, both in terms of personal growth and spiritual development. These practices are often rooted in various spiritual and cultural traditions and can help you in the following ways:

* **Self-Discovery:** Goddess embodiment practices encourage self-reflection and self-discovery. By exploring and connecting with different goddess archetypes, you can better understand different facets of your personality and psyche.
* **Empowerment:** Many goddess archetypes represent powerful and empowering qualities, such as strength, wisdom, and creativity. Engaging with these archetypes can help you tap into your own inner strength and confidence.
* **Healing:** Goddess embodiment practices often involve rituals and meditation techniques that can aid in emotional and spiritual healing. They provide a safe space for processing and releasing past traumas and negative emotions.
* **Divine Feminine Energy**: These practices often focus on embracing and celebrating feminine energy. This can be especially beneficial for people who identify as women, as it can help them connect with their feminine essence and embrace it fully.
* **Balance:** Embodying different goddess archetypes can help restore balance in your life. For example, if you tend to be overly analytical and logical, connecting with a goddess archetype that embodies intuition and creativity can help bring balance to your life.
* **Connection with Nature**: Many goddesses are associated with aspects of nature, such as the moon, earth, or water. Engaging in goddess embodiment practices can deepen your connection with the natural world and promote environmental consciousness.
* **Spiritual Growth:** These practices often have a spiritual component, allowing you to deepen your connection with the divine or your chosen spiritual path. They can provide a sense of purpose and a greater understanding of the sacred.
* **Community and Support:** Engaging in goddess embodiment practices can lead you to connect with like-minded individuals who share similar interests and beliefs. This sense of community can provide emotional support and a sense of belonging.
* **Self-Care:** Many goddess embodiment practices emphasize self-care and self-love. They encourage you to prioritize your well-being, both physically and emotionally.
* **Ritual and Ceremony:** Goddess embodiment practices often involve rituals and ceremonies that can provide structure and meaning to your life. These rituals can be grounding and help you create a sense of order and purpose.

It's important to note that goddess embodiment practices vary widely depending on the tradition or path you follow. Some people may engage in elaborate rituals, while others may incorporate these practices into their daily lives through meditation, journaling, or other personal rituals. Ultimately, the benefits you derive from goddess embodiment practices will depend on your personal beliefs, intentions, and commitment to the practice.
