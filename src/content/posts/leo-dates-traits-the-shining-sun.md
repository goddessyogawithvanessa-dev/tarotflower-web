Leo, represented by the majestic lion, spans from July 23rd to August 22nd. This fire sign exudes warmth, passion, and a natural flair for the dramatic. Ruled by the Sun, the center of our solar system, Leos shine brightly wherever they go. They possess a vibrant energy that lights up any room and captivates those around them.

Leos are known for their charismatic and outgoing nature. They thrive on attention and love being the center of the stage. Their enthusiasm is infectious, and they have a natural ability to inspire and motivate others. Leos are courageous and confident, always ready to take on new challenges and lead the way with their natural leadership skills.

As a fire sign, Leos are passionate and driven individuals. They have a strong sense of purpose and are not afraid to pursue their goals and dreams. They are creative and artistic, often expressing themselves through various forms of self-expression, whether it be art, music, or performance.

The Strength tarot card is associated with Leo, symbolizing their inner power, courage, and resilience. It represents the ability to harness their strength to overcome obstacles and challenges. The Strength card reminds Leos to channel their energy in a positive and constructive way, embracing their personal power while remaining compassionate and gentle.

With their ruling planet being the Sun, Leos are radiant and full of life. They bring warmth and joy wherever they go, lighting up the lives of those around them. Just as the Sun is the center of the solar system, Leos have a natural magnetism that draws people in. They are natural leaders and have a generous and loving heart that makes them fiercely loyal to their loved ones.

In summary, Leos are passionate, charismatic, and confident individuals who have an undeniable presence. They possess a natural leadership ability and a strong sense of purpose. Embodying the Strength tarot card, they tap into their inner power and shine brightly, inspiring others with their warmth and generosity.

## The Leo Tarot Card

![strength tarot card meaning, major arcana, rider waite](/images/2022/03/strength-rw.jpeg)

The Leo tarot card is associated with the Strength card in the Major Arcana. This card embodies the powerful and confident nature of the Leo zodiac sign. Represented by the symbol of a woman taming a lion in the [Rider Waite](https://amzn.to/3BFR4Ii) deck, it symbolizes courage, inner strength, and resilience. The Strength card calls upon individuals to tap into their inner power and embrace their unique qualities. It highlights the importance of self-belief, assertiveness, and unwavering determination in navigating life's challenges.

The Strength card is also linked to the element of fire, which perfectly aligns with Leo's fiery and passionate nature. Fire represents creativity, inspiration, and a zest for life. It ignites the Leo's energy, ambition, and enthusiasm. The Leo tarot card encourages individuals to harness this fiery energy in a positive and balanced way, using it to fuel their passions and manifest their desires. It reminds individuals to approach life with boldness and conviction, trusting in their own abilities to overcome obstacles and achieve greatness.

In a tarot reading, the Leo card signifies a time of personal empowerment and self-discovery. It invites individuals to embrace their authenticity and shine their light brightly. It encourages them to step into leadership roles, express their creativity, and take charge of their destiny. The Leo tarot card reminds individuals to be courageous in pursuing their dreams and to use their strength and passion to make a positive impact in the world.
