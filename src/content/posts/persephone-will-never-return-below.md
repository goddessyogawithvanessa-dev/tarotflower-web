#### **The High Priestess and Pluto: Ten Steps From Darkness to Light**

Most importantly of all, as much as we might like to indulge ourselves in a bit of Schadenfreude, Lady Justice is compassionate and fair. The scales will be brought into balance, nothing more, for revenge, is not what she seeks. It is up to those that fell into the underworld to decide how hard Lady Justice will have to push back to regain equilibrium.

Only they can decide whether to ascend or stay below. But the decision will be final. Persephone will not be returning to the underworld ever again. This Spring will be eternal for those of us above ground. Even if fires burn around me, I will charge forward in the name of the light. Let's live joyfully on the side of bliss.
