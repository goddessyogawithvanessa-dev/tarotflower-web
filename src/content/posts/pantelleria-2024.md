## ABOUT PANTELLERIA

There's a reason why you've probably never heard of Pantelleria - it's a secret getaway for the likes of Giorgio Armani and Madonna. Located in the middle of the Mediterranean between Sicily and Tunisia, this elegant, mysterious, and wild island, boasts natural hot springs, sailing, rugged terrain and rooftop dining. Get "Primal in Pantelleria," with us, through daily yoga, dance, ocean and hot spring activities, wine tasting, and a dive into all of Pantelleria's natural beauty and adventures.
