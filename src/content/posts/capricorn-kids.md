### **Children with the Ascendant, Sun and/or moon in Capricorn**

### **Birthstones**: Turquoise/Garnet

### **Ruling Planet:** Saturn

### **Capricorn Rules the 10th House**

### **Earth sign**

Capricorn kids are **practical** but **ambitious**, **down-to-earth** yet **persistent**, and appreciate the simple things in life. They’re ruled by slow moving **Saturn**, so they take their time to process information in a gradual methodical manner. They’re like little goats climbing up a hill slow and steady.

Capricorn children frequently come across obstacles, making mistakes along the way, but are quick to learn from them and continue relentlessly moving forward. Some might underestimate them during the process, but once they’ve reached the mountain top, they are untouchable.

## All About The Earth Signs

In astrology, the Earth signs are Taurus, Virgo and Capricorn. They are the physical ***manifesters*** of the zodiac. Earth signs are **calm**,  **practical**, **authentic**, **productive** and **dependable** **perfectionists**. They also LOVE **routines** and **organization** which allows them to finish what they start, achieving tangible results and longevity.

## How To Support Your Little Capricorn

So, for parents who have little mountain climbers, it is important to have patience and guide them in their process of reaching their mountain top. Go ahead and encourage them to think critically by asking them for solutions to practical problems. This will encourage them to develop this innate talent. You would be surprised at the depth of wisdom that little cappies possess!

## Capricorn Kids: A Cardinal Sign

The Cardinal signs of the zodiac are comprised of **Aries**, **Cancer**, **Libra** and **Capricorn**. They fall under the changing of the seasons. Therefore, being born under this shift from one season to the other, makes  them the ***initiators*** of the zodiac. They are **goal-oriented** and full of **ideas**.

The sign of Capricorn represents the shift of Fall to Winter. Children born under this period are built to weather the storm. They frequently encounter hurdles to overcome early in life, which causes them to grow up fast. But this also endows them with a high capacity for overcoming obstacles and taking initiative.

Combine the practical, ambitious and meticulous nature of an earth sign, with the visionary qualities of the Cardinal signs, and you've got a powerhouse in the making! These traits create the potential for your Capricorn to achieve whatever he or she sets their mind to!

## Capricorn Kids and Career

Capricorn rules the 10th house of the zodiac, which represents **government**, **careers**, and one's **status** in the world. So, Capricorns have an aptitude for aiming and achieving a very high status in what they do. Capricorn children often grow up to become successful entrepreneurs or CEO's. Therefore, don’t be surprised if your Capricorn child already knows what he/she wants to do when they grow up!

## Parenting Tips for Capricorn Kids

Don’t be afraid to make Capricorn kids earn what they desire, because this will stimulate their self-motivation and prepare them to eventually tackle more difficult tasks independently! Capricorn kids love a challenge, so give them an objective to achieve like creating a lemonade stand or being responsible for doing certain chores around the house to earn money. This will help them to develop discipline, a solid work ethic, and will help them to understand the concept of saving money. Plus, this will also build their self-confidence when they see the material results from their hard work!

## The Personality of Capricorn Kids

Capricorns demonstrate **wisdom** beyond their years and have an **mature** demeanor. As children, they frequently get along well in company of older people. Capricorns can come across as cold and unemotional at times but in actuality they are very **shy**. However, they like being social and often appreciate the company of extrovert people.

Capricorn kids are perfectionists and take it personally when they aren’t able to fulfill the high expectations that they put upon themselves. Therefore, it’s important to provide a fun and light-hearted atmosphere for Capricorn kiddies. Capricorns also appreciate traditions, so take them to local events and cultural experiences linked to their traditional norms. They will really appreciate it!
