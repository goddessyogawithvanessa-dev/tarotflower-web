Maiden Moon, manifest dreams as I tread the tender seams

Walking gently 'tween dark and light, carry the torch into the night

Cast away the old from the new

Sew together what follows through

May the seeds of dreams unfurl as I proudly walk this world

So mote it be, Maiden Moon.
