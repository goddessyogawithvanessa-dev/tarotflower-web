They are known for being great listeners and providing emotional support to those they care about.

As a Cancer, they tend to have a warm and inviting personality. They are highly empathetic and attuned to the emotions of others. Cancer individuals are highly protective of those they care about, and they will do anything to keep them safe and secure. They are also highly imaginative and creative, which can make them excellent artists, writers, and musicians.

In astrology, Cancer is ruled by the Moon, which is associated with emotions and intuition.
