## The Temperance Tarot Card Summarized

![Temperance tarot card meaning major arcana Midjourney art by Vanessa Hylande](/images/2022/02/vanessahylande_a_beautiful_woman_an_angel_holding_a_cup_of_wate_84d1f18f-81d5-4f90-9b9a-187a5c58bd53.jpg)

In a tarot reading, The Temperance tarot card is a card of peace and harmony. It expresses the need for moderation in your life and the need to slow down. Moreover, it urges you to take the middle road and allow your life to flow no matter what comes your way. It is also a sign that it is time to know what you value and to set yourself goals. In other words, it asks you to concentrate on what is important and to begin living the life you believe in. This card asks you to dive deep into yourself and broaden your horizons.

This card also indicates that there are areas and situations of your life in which you need to find balance, and that you should learn to remain calm during moments of distress. No matter how complicated the situation is, you need to maintain a sense of calm even if your emotions are pushing you to react immediately. Patience, flexibility and calmness are some qualities you must have in order to manifest what you truly desire. This way, you'll be able to live with great joy and relief. The Temperance tarot card teaches you to put your trust in the divine timing of the world.

## Temperance in a Twin Flame Reading

In a twin flame reading, the Temperance tarot card holds a special significance, bringing a message of balance, harmony, and patience. This card serves as a gentle reminder that the twin flame journey is a process that requires a steady and patient approach.

Temperance encourages you to find the middle ground and seek moderation in your twin flame connection. It reminds you to blend the energies of love, compassion, and understanding with patience and self-control. By maintaining a balanced approach, you can navigate the ups and downs of the journey with grace and serenity.

The presence of the Temperance card in a twin flame reading also signifies the importance of harmony within yourself. It reminds you to find inner balance and align your mind, body, and spirit. By nurturing your own well-being and practicing self-care, you create a solid foundation for a harmonious union with your twin flame.

Embracing the energy of Temperance in your twin flame journey brings comfort and reassurance. It encourages you to trust the divine timing and flow of the connection. Just as the alchemist blends different elements to create something new, Temperance invites you to blend the lessons, experiences, and energies of the twin flame journey to create a beautiful and transformative union.

Remember, dear friend, that the twin flame journey is not a race but a journey of self-discovery and growth. The presence of the Temperance card reminds you to be patient, maintain balance, and trust in the divine process. Trust that everything is unfolding as it should and that you are on the path towards a harmonious and fulfilling connection with your twin flame.

## The Temperance Tarot Card in the Past Position

In the past position, the Temperance card expresses that you have had a harmonious and well-balanced life. It can be reflective of your childhood or your whole upbringing. In addition, Temperance tarot indicates that you may have needed to cope with a challenging situation with composure recently. Alternatively, perhaps you have missed the opportunity to take advantage of a situation. You are where you are now as you have chosen to temper your emotions and managed to strive toward harmonization.

## The Temperance Tarot Card in the Present Position

The Temperance in the present position expresses that you need to be able to control yourself              especially when having a dispute. You may be facing troubles and challenges currently. However, this tarot card advises you to try to find common ground and to handle the current situation in harmony and peace whatever happens. If you do so, you soon see a positive outcome manifesting.

## The Temperance Tarot Card in the Future Position

In the future position, the Temperance tarot card signifies that there will be relief, peace and well-being, even though things might not be going right at the current moment.

And remember, it is not good to stress about things too much or get frustrated. Instead, try to bring yourself back into balance. For instance, you can consider taking your attention in another atmosphere, or doing meditation and some energy work.

## The Temperance Tarot Card in the Reverse

![Temperance tarot card meaning major arcana Midjourney art by Vanessa Hylande](/images/2022/02/vanessahylande_a_beautiful_woman_an_angel_pouring_two_cups_of_w_0688b342-43f4-4263-bbf2-ee17f9ff20ae.jpg)

In the reverse, the Temperance tarot card appearing in a reading is a sign of imbalances, excess, indulgence and need for re-alignment. It indicates that something is out of balance right now and it is high time to restore moderation into your life, and that, as soon as possible.  In fact, you may have been over-eating, over-drinking, or engaging in other excesses.

Perhaps you have also spent too much time thinking about things in a negative way or having disputes with other people. Some areas of your life, either personal or professional, may not flow as smoothly as you had expected, or something may just feel off about your current path.

### How to Implement the Lessons of the Temperance Card in Reverse

Therefore, when you receive this tarot card in reverse in a reading, know that it invites you to listen to and trust your inner voice. Listen to yourself and to what you want deep in your heart. Your inner voice may tell you something you do not even want to hear, but you had better care. The heart wants what it wants, and when do do not listen to the voice of the heart, it can manifest in our world as stress, anxiety, anger or depression.

Take a moment to honestly evaluate how things are going in your life right now and re-align your daily life with what you truly desire. More precisely, align yourself with your highest goals. While doing so, do not forget to think in the long term, always, and even more importantly, balance the flow of your inner world with with the physical. Additionally, it is not a good idea to make hasty decisions, because it can make the situation even worse and more complicated. So practice patience, calm and serenity.

## Symbology of the Temperance Tarot Card

[![](/images/2022/02/Minor-arcana-1.jpg)](https://amzn.to/3M48pzC)

The whole scenery of the Temperance Tarot Card depicts peace, harmony, and balance. In fact, it features a genderless and white-dressed angel pouring water from one cup to another. Te angel is also dipping one of her feet in water, as the other one stands on the shore.

The fact that the angel has no gender expresses neutrality of sexes and equality. The white robe, with a triangle within a square embroidered on it on the chest, is symbolic of union and healing. The white robe is actually a sign of clarity of the mind.

The gesture of pouring water between two cups equally, is a symbol of life’s flow and balance. Having only one foot in the water and the other on the land implies balance between unconscious realm of water and conscious realm of the earth. There is a perfect harmony in this scenario, as the angel of Temperance is serene and calm; it expresses neither sadness nor happiness.

In the background, we can see blossoming flowers, beautiful mountains, lands and vegetation. All of the symbolism found in this artwork represent the highs and lows of life. Both the sun and the halo represent the enlightenment that creates harmony.

## How to Embody the Temperance Tarot Card

![temperane tarot card, affirmations](/images/2022/02/5.jpg)

As we can see, the Temperance Tarot Card is all about balance and the peaceful flow we can achieve on the condition that we embrace moderation. However, it is more than just a card directing us toward harmony and self-moderation.

Temperance also teaches you to balance your lifestyle with your goals. You may wonder how to do so! The most authentic way to do so is to ask yourself what you really want in life. It is better to be more specific, so you should have an idea of where you want to be in five days, five weeks, five months, and even five years. Now that you know what you want to do, what you have to do next is to ask yourself why you want those things. In fact, you must know the why behind your actions and desires. When dealing with this question, always answer honestly and carefully. This is the only way for us to truly align with our heart's desire.

### Embodying the Temperance Tarot Card with Practical Choices

It is important to determine what in your life is serving your highest good and interests. Only then can you plan to make changes to accommodate your inner truth. Whether there are people in your life that are pulling you from your life path, or whether there are physical situations or responsibilities that are not serving your highest good, the Temperance tarot card requires you to be completely honest with yourself.

Temperance urges you to give up what no longer serves you, despite the fact that you may still want it or believe you need or must continue with it. There is no need to be afraid. The Temperance tarot card teaches you how to give up those things that are self-limiting, while sustaining and nurturing growing what serves your best.

Remember that you may need to make small changes and adjustments incrementally. We cannot deny that perhaps, it will not be that easy to learn to let go of pleasure and comforts that do not enrich us. But we must we keep in mind is that the uncomfortable process itself can bring self-satisfaction and make room for deeper insights.

### Embodying the Temperance Tarot Card Through Honest Self-Reflection

[![temperance tarot](/images/2022/02/IMG_20220216_123043-1024x1016.jpg)](https://amzn.to/3MniCIy)

The Temperance tarot card is also a sign that you need to look at some of the things about yourself that you would like to change. For example, your emotions, personality, habits, reactions, etc. You must be real with yourself. For instance, you may not be so proud of yourself when you are not able to hold back from quick judgment or you cannot resist the temptation to react immediately when you are having dispute and quarrels with your friends or relatives.

Do not judge or shame yourself, but commit to self-improvement with self-love and self-care. Yes, you may need to learn to think for before acting and to live in a way that you do not break others' hearts. Although it may take time and self-discipline, by doing so, you do not only show respect for others, but also respect for yourself.

### Embodying The Temperance Tarot Card Through Moderation

Another interesting aspect of the Temperance Tarot card is that it teaches us that by giving up the chaos of excess, it is possible to access deeper parts of ourselves. This means that we can handle something successfully as it is supposed to be without depending on distractions or self-numbing behaviors and addictions. Temperance teaches us to avoid temptation, whatever it is, and not let it lead us to the road of overindulgence. We have to resist temptation with a higher purpose in mind.

In conclusion, in order to embody the Temperance tarot card, you need to embody the skills and qualities we have learned above and put them into practice in your daily life. Try to show through your way of living that you are an instrument of peace. That you behave in a way that enables you to create perfect harmony in your life. Practice diligence and continue to tell yourself that you will always strive toward harmonization, and will not stop until you are sure your life is in harmony and balance. This way, you live your life with happiness and you always know how you should react no matter what may come your way.
