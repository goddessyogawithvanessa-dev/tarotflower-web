It draws inspiration from the wisdom of nature, particularly the [white sage plant](https://amzn.to/3Lxcc9i) which is known for its cleansing and purifying properties. White sage is often used in smudging, particularly as an ancient Native American traditions to cleanse spaces and people,

The overall theme of the White Sage Tarot Deck in a tin is one of mindfulness, healing, and spiritual evolution, making it an ideal tool for those seeking to deepen their spiritual practice or explore their own inner landscape.
