If you’ve ever had the Five of Cups appear in a Tarot reading, you know that it can be an incredibly powerful and emotional card. This card is associated with loss, regret, and disappointment, but it also carries with it the potential for healing and hope.

<img src="/images/tarot/cups/five-of-cups.png" alt="Veiled figure walking away from five overturned cups toward a distant castle" width="1024" height="1024" loading="eager" decoding="async" />

The Five of Cups tarot card represents a period of sorrow and grief. This card often appears when we’ve experienced a great loss and are feeling overwhelmed with sadness. It can also signal a period of transition, where we are letting go of something or someone that we once held dear. It is a reminder that endings can bring new beginnings, and that while we may feel the pain of loss, it is ultimately a necessary part of growth.

The imagery on the card can provide further insight into its meaning. It typically depicts a figure standing amidst three spilled cups, while two full cups remain upright behind them. This can indicate that while we may have experienced a loss, there are still things in our life that remain intact. It is a reminder that although we may be experiencing a difficult time, there is still hope and possibility ahead.

The Five of Cups tarot card also represents the need to accept our feelings and move on. This card often appears when we are stuck in the past and struggling to let go. It is a reminder to move forward and find peace in our situation. We can use this card as a reminder to forgive ourselves and others, and to find solace in the knowledge that even though things may not be perfect, we can still find joy and happiness in life.

Overall, the Five of Cups is a powerful card that can bring about a deep transformation. It can signal a period of sadness and sorrow, but it also carries the potential for new beginnings and hope. It is a reminder that although we may experience loss and pain, it is ultimately a part of the journey that leads to growth and healing.

## The five of cups love

The Five of Cups tarot card in a love reading can indicate a feeling of loss in the relationship. This card can signify a period of grief over a past relationship, or a feeling of regret over something that was said or done. This card can also indicate that there is an imbalance in the relationship, and that one person is giving more than they are receiving. To move forward in the relationship, focus on rebuilding trust and communication to create a more balanced and equitable relationship.

## The Five of Cups Reversed

The Five of Cups reversed can signal that grief is beginning to move and that acceptance is becoming possible. You may be ready to forgive, reconnect with life, or recognize the support that remained present through a difficult season. It does not erase what was lost. Instead, it suggests that sorrow no longer has to be the only thing you can see.

## How to Embody the Five of Cups

Allow grief to have a shape and a place: write what you miss, speak it aloud, or create a small ritual of release. Then turn gently toward what remains. Embodying this card is the practice of holding loss and gratitude together without asking either one to cancel the other.
