The Fool tarot card, the first card of the [Major Arcana](https://tarotflower.com/major-arcana-2/), is a card of new beginnings. Like the crescent moon or the morning star, it signals a surrender to what the future may hold. Taking that first step toward your new beginning.

If you've been feeling stuck or unsure, you're going to be really happy to receive this card! This **fun-loving** archetype represents **new beginnings**, the start of a **new life**, or a **new journey**.

In the [Rider-Waite](https://amzn.to/430oVaA) Tarot deck, The Fool tarot card, the young man is taking his first steps onto the path of **spiritual ascension**.

The Fool dances through life without a care in the world. He lets his free spirit guide him. He doesn't let fear stop him and he has left the past behind. Drawing The Fool card is a message of **hope** and **possibility**, so get ready for a whole new world!

## The Fool Meaning

![the fool tarot card meaning, major arcana, the fool card, Midjourney art by Vanessa Hylande](/images/2023/05/AI-Square-2.jpg)

The name of The Fool tarot card can be a little misleading. Getting this card in your tarot card spread doesn't mean that you've been foolish! This card is really about **surrender** and **abandon**. Allowing yourself to just "go for it" without trying to control the outcome.

Depending on this tarot card's placement in your spread, and also on the surrounding cards, it can mean different things. But the theme is always the same: The Fool tarot card embodies a willingness to just "**jump in**." Are you ready to answer the call?

## The Fool Tarot Card in the Past

We always have to consider where the cards land. The Fool card in the past position shows us that you recently has a significant new beginning in your life. Maybe you started a new job, or relationship, or some other kind of project. Whatever the case may be, this new beginning was important in your life and is influencing your situation today.

## The Fool Tarot Card in the Present

In the present position, The Fool tarot card is encouraging you to **take a leap of faith**! If you are a little bit unsure about whether or not you should take a risk on something, this card is telling you "yes!" Go for it if it feels right in your heart, and don't let fear get in the way of your dreams!

## The Fool Tarot Card in the Future

The Fool in the future position indicates that you are going to need to **take a chance** on something in the near future! Whatever is coming your way may require you to step out of your comfort zone a little. But don't worry! This card represents **infinite possibility**, and your courage will be met with **happiness** and **new opportunities**. Let go of fear and resistance and **trust in the universe**.

## The Fool Tarot Card Meaning Love

If you're happily in partnership, the Fool says you and your partner are about to have a **new beginning** together. Maybe your relationship has felt a little stuck lately. Well, that is about to change! If you're single, it looks like you have some opportunities headed your way, but you're going to have to **put yourself out there**! Nobody ever found love sitting at home, so go ahead and try something new - even if it is only virtually!

The archetype of The Fool card has everything he needs with him. He's carrying his belongings over his shoulder, his little dog is by his side, and he has a big smile upon his face.

Remember that you have everything you need in love too, and step out into the world with the same smile on your face.

## The Fool Reversed

Card reversals always indicate that the qualities of this archetype are out of balance. This can mean they are either in excess or are lacking. Are you engaging in risky, careless behavior? Or are you so worried about making a mistake that you're not moving toward your dreams at all?

Either way, a reversal of this card is asking you to take a step back and reflect on the traits of The Fool, and then take action to bring these traits into harmony and alignment.

## The Fool in the Rider Waite

![the fool tarot card rider waite](/images/2021/01/Fool-rw.jpeg)

In the original Rider-Waite, The Fool tarot card depicts a young man walking gingerly upon a mountain path with a rucksack over his shoulder and a little white dog by his side.

He doesn't seem to have a care in the world and he isn't worrying about what lies before him. He holds a single while rose in his hand, a symbol of purity and innocence. His little dog is looking out for him, the way that spirit guides often do.
