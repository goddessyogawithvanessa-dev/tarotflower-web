## Divine Feminine Affirmations for Kundalini Awakening and Goddess Embodiment

My body is my temple and it is beautiful

I am full of love and light and can manifest anything I desire

My intuition is my guiding light, leading me along the sacred path of my divine purpose

I am a vessel of love, compassion, and creativity

The goddess within me is wise, strong, and nurturing

I trust the rhythm of my body and honor its natural cycles

My sensuality is a gift, and I express it with grace and confidence

I release self-doubt and embrace my inner strength

My emotions are a source of power, and I allow myself to feel deeply

I am in tune with the cycles of nature, and I flow with life effortlessly

I create sacred space within and around me, inviting love and abundance

Every step I take is a dance of self-love and self-acceptance

I honor the divine feminine energy in all living beings

My voice is a melody of empowerment and authenticity

I am a creator, manifesting my desires with the energy of the goddess

I trust the process of life, knowing that I am supported and guided

I radiate beauty and grace from the inside out

I am a channel for divine love and healing energy

I embrace the ebb and flow of life, finding balance in every moment

I am a beacon of light, inspiring others to embrace their divine feminine essence

I am a vessel of transformation, fearlessly facing change with the power of Goddess Kali

Every step I take is a dance of cosmic energy, aligning me with the divine flow of life

The sacred energy of Kundalini rises within me, opening and aligning my chakras

I trust in the unfolding of my spiritual journey, surrendering to the wisdom of the divine

My heart is a wellspring of compassion, and I extend love and kindness to all beings

I am a sacred being, embodying the delicate balance of strength and gentleness

I attract and manifest positive energy, creating a life filled with joy and fulfillment

I am a channel for divine wisdom, receiving insights and guidance from the higher realms

My thoughts, words, and actions align with the divine grace that flows through me

I am a beacon of light, illuminating the path for myself and others on this journey

I am one with the universe, a divine expression of love, grace, and the eternal beauty of existence
