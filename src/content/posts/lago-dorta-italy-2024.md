## ABOUT LAGO D'ORTA, ITALY

Lago d’Orta in Italy is a stunning lake located in the Italian Alps and has some of the most breathtaking views you can imagine. The lake is surrounded by the mountain range and villages, giving you a unique sense of serenity and peace. This special place has an ancient lineage connected to mysticism and feminine energy.

## FAQ'S

No, transportation from the airport is not included but we can help you to arrange a ride. The simplest is to take an uber or taxi directly to Casa dei Padri.

You will definitely need cash. Most restaurants and small gelaterias do not accept credit cards, and there are not a lot of ATMs in the area. We recommend you convert your currency to Euro before you leave or at the airport.

The weather is typically sunny and warm, though because we are situated in the Alps, there are occasional showers. It can also get quite chilly in the evening so bring some warm comfy clothes along with your swimsuit!

We provide yoga mats, bolsters and blankets for classes, but if you prefer to bring your own, you are welcome to. If you require any additional specialty items such as belts and blocks, we recommend that you bring them with you.

Aside from your regular yoga wear, pack comfortable shoes and clothes for walking/hiking. Definitely bring your swimsuit and something nicer for evening dinners if you like to dress up! As stated previously, bring some warm cover ups for the evening chill and also pack an umbrella or rain jacket in case of rain.

Sunscreen, sunglasses, insect repellent, umbrella, sneakers, flip flops or sandals for the beach.

There is a small grocery store next door to the retreat center and also a pharmacy a few blocks away. Additionally, you can enjoy a local cafe, bakeries, pizzerias and small restaurants.
