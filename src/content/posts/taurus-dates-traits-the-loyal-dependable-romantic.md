Welcome to the world of Taurus - one of the most dependable and practical zodiac signs! If you were born between April 20 and May 20, you fall under the Taurus astrological sign, also known as the Taurus zodiac sign or Taurus star sign.

People born under this sign are known for their grounded nature, strong work ethic, and steadfast personality. Taurus personality traits include being reliable, patient, and persistent. They are also known for their love of beauty, comfort, and luxury, making them great connoisseurs of art, music, and food.

In terms of Taurus zodiac compatibility, they are most compatible with other earth signs such as Virgo and Capricorn, as well as water signs like Cancer and Pisces. However, Taurus can be quite stubborn, which can sometimes create challenges in relationships.

If you have a Taurus birthday coming up, expect to be celebrated by your loved ones who appreciate your steady and loyal presence in their lives. So, whether you are a Taurus yourself or just curious about this fascinating zodiac sign, let's dive into the world of Taurus astrology together!

4. **Practical:** Taurus individuals are grounded and practical in their approach to life. They have a sensible and down-to-earth nature, which helps them make sound decisions based on practicality and common sense.

7. **Stubborn:** Taurus can be quite stubborn at times, often displaying a strong determination to stick to their opinions and beliefs. They may resist change or be hesitant to adapt to new circumstances.
8. **Grounded:** Taurus individuals have a grounded and practical nature, which helps them stay rooted and stable in their lives. They often seek stability and security in their relationships and surroundings.
9. **Appreciative of beauty:** Taurus individuals have a deep appreciation for beauty in all its forms. They are drawn to aesthetics and have an eye for art, design, and the finer things in life.
10. **Materialistic:** Taurus individuals have a tendency to place value on material possessions and financial security. They appreciate the finer things in life and may work hard to acquire material comforts and stability.
