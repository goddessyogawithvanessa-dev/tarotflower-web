Scorpio is a dynamic and intense zodiac sign known for its transformative energy. It spans from **October 23 to November 21** and is associated with several unique traits. Ruled by the planet Pluto, Scorpios are passionate, resourceful, and determined individuals. They possess a deep emotional intelligence and are often drawn to uncovering hidden truths. The Tarot card associated with Scorpio is [Death](https://tarotflower.com/tarot-card-meanings/death-tarot/), which represents profound transformation, letting go of the old, and embracing new beginnings. Scorpio is characterized by the element of Water, symbolizing their depth of emotions and intuitive nature.

What sets Scorpio apart is its connection to three (or 5 depending on the interpretation) spirit animals that represent different stages of evolution. The Scorpion signifies the initial stage, representing the intense and protective nature of Scorpios. The second stage is represented by the Eagle, symbolizing their ability to rise above challenges and gain a broader perspective. The Phoenix represents the third stage, embodying the transformative aspect of Scorpio as they rise from the ashes, letting go of the past and experiencing rebirth.

## The Three Incarnations of the Scorpio Zodiac Sign

![the great eagle, the Hierophant tarot card meaning, the shaman, scorpio zodiac, spirit animal, Midjourney art by Vanessa Hylande](/images/2021/02/hieroph-14.jpg)

In astrology, Scorpio is associated with three distinct incarnations, representing different stages of its evolution. These incarnations symbolize the growth and transformation of the Scorpio energy:

1. **The Scorpion:** At the initial stage, Scorpio is represented by the Scorpion. This incarnation embodies the primal instincts and intense passions of Scorpio. The Scorpion is fiercely protective and can be both secretive and vengeful when provoked.
2. **The Eagle:** As Scorpio progresses, it transitions into the incarnation of the Eagle. The Eagle represents a higher level of awareness and the ability to rise above challenges. It signifies the development of wisdom, insight, and a broader perspective. The Eagle incarnation encourages Scorpio to use their power and intensity for spiritual growth.
3. **The Phoenix:** The Phoenix is the embodiment of transformation and rebirth. It symbolizes the burning away of the old and the rising from the ashes. As Scorpio reaches this stage, it undergoes a profound metamorphosis, shedding old patterns and beliefs. The Phoenix teaches Scorpio the power of regeneration, resilience, and rising stronger from the depths of darkness.

### The final incarnation of Scorpio

![Scorpio dates and traits, scorpio compatibility zodiac astrology midjourney art Vanessa Hylande](/images/2023/04/scorpio-1.jpg)

There are two other spirit animals that are often associated with the Scorpio once this zodiac sign has has embodied the Phoenix Rising: the Serpent and the Dove. The Serpent represents the wisdom, healing, and transformative power of Scorpio. It embodies the Kundalini energy, symbolizing the awakening of spiritual and creative energy within. It sheds its skin, symbolizing the shedding of old patterns and embracing a new self. The Dove is associated with purity, peace, and spiritual awakening. In this context, it represents the purification and spiritual liberation that Scorpio experiences as it transcends its previous limitations and emerges into a higher state of consciousness.

Ultimately, whether the Phoenix is associated with the dove or the serpent depends on the specific interpretation and symbolism used by different traditions and individuals. Both symbols highlight the transformative nature of Scorpio's evolution and the potential for profound spiritual growth and rebirth.

## Scorpio Element: Water

![Death tarot card meaning, scorpio zodiac, Major Arcana, Midjourney art by Vanessa Hylande](/images/2022/02/vanessahylande_a_gorgeous_woman_with_black_hair_with_piercing_b_dda55fc9-4671-49db-af77-f737fe0647fa.jpg)

Scorpio is a zodiac sign associated with the water element, which reflects their deep emotional nature and intense personality. As a water sign, Scorpios possess the following characteristics:

Scorpios are known for their profound emotional depth and sensitivity. They experience emotions with great intensity and are highly attuned to the emotional currents around them. This depth of emotion allows them to connect deeply with others on an empathic level.

They have a strong intuitive sense and heightened empathy, which enables them to understand the unspoken needs and emotions of others. Scorpios are perceptive and insightful, often picking up on subtle cues and energies. This intuitive nature makes them supportive and compassionate friends.

Scorpios are adaptable and possess a remarkable ability to undergo personal transformation. They embrace change and are willing to face their deepest fears and shadows, which allows them to grow and evolve. Their resilience and ability to rise from challenging situations make them strong individuals.

In relationships, Scorpios seek profound connections and crave emotional intimacy and loyalty. They value trust and honesty above all else and can be fiercely protective of their loved ones. However, they are selective in whom they choose to trust, as they need to feel a strong emotional bond and a sense of safety in their connections.

Scorpios are naturally drawn to the mysterious and the unseen. They have a keen sense of intuition that guides them in navigating complex situations. Their intuitive nature often leads them to uncover hidden truths and delve into the realms of the unknown.

Overall, as a water sign, Scorpio's emotional depth, intuition, adaptability, and transformative nature make them complex and intriguing individuals.

## Scorpio Tarot: Death

![death tarot card meaning rider waite, major arcana](/images/2023/05/Rider-Waite-3-1024x1024.jpg)

The Scorpio tarot card in the Rider-Waite deck is represented by the Death card. This card depicts a skeleton wearing black armor, riding a white horse. In the background, there is a rising sun, symbolizing new beginnings. The Death card in the tarot does not represent physical death but rather signifies transformation, endings, and rebirth.

The Scorpio tarot card carries powerful symbolism, reflecting the transformative nature of the zodiac sign. It represents the need to release and let go of old patterns, beliefs, and situations that no longer serve your growth and evolution. It symbolizes the death of the old self and the birth of a new, more authentic self. This card encourages embracing change and transformation, even if it feels uncomfortable or challenging.

The Scorpio tarot card is associated with deep emotional and psychological transformations. It signifies the need to confront fears, face inner shadows, and delve into the depths of your own psyche. It urges you to embrace the process of inner healing, self-discovery, and personal growth. Through this transformation, you can experience profound emotional and spiritual liberation.

In a tarot reading, the Scorpio card often signifies the need to release attachments, let go of the past, and embrace new beginnings. It invites you to surrender to the natural cycles of life and embrace the transformative power of change. This card encourages you to trust the process and have faith that the endings you experience will lead to new opportunities and growth.

The Scorpio tarot card serves as a reminder that transformation and growth are essential parts of life's journey. It encourages you to face your fears, embrace change, and allow yourself to be reborn in a more authentic and empowered state. By embracing the transformative energy of the Scorpio card, you can tap into your inner strength, resilience, and capacity for personal evolution.
