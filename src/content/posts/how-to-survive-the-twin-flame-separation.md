Is your twin flame invading your psychic space? Are you feeling unwanted connections and emotions that are driving you crazy? Are you in twin flame separation trying to forget your twin, but you just can't seem to cut the cord? Is it obsession? [Is it all in your head?](https://tarotflower.com/twin-flame/twin-flames-no-youre-not-crazy-why-sex-with-your-twin-is-so-mind-blowing/) Why is this happening and how can you get past it? Here are some helpful tips to navigate, survive and *thrive* a twin flame separation:

## Self-care and personal growth

I know it can be frustrating to hear, but focusing on your own growth and personal transformation is really the key to your personal evolution and your twin flame relationship. You may think that you have already done the work, and it is tempting to look to the outside to fill what's missing: "I just want someone to love me, to make me feel safe, to provide security, make me feel loved and beautiful." But ask yourself: "What is it that my twin flame evokes in me that sparks my inner light? What are the feelings and inspiration that come to me through our connection?" Then try to find ways to create those experiences and emotions for yourself. You must create safety, love, beauty, security for yourself.

Use this time apart to invest in your own well-being and personal development. Engage in activities that nourish your mind, body, and soul. Practice self-care rituals, explore new hobbies, and engage in healing practices that resonate with you. Prioritize your own growth and self-discovery during this transformative period.

## Trust in the love and connection

![twin flame separation, divine feminine healing, twin flame sex, twin flame union](/images/2023/06/twin-flame-union-3.jpg)

Although the twin flame separation may not be temporary, it is important to remember that the love is real. Sometimes we are left wondering if we are crazy and if it's all in our heads. But the truth is that the love is real in the 5D, whether it is able to manifest in the 3D at this point in time or not. The important thing is to shift the focus away from your twin and onto what you are still lacking. Your twin is your mirror soul, and the separation is teaching you things that you still need to learn.

Have faith in the deep love and connection you share with your twin flame. Trust that this separation is a part of the journey, allowing both of you to evolve individually and work through any unresolved issues. Hold onto the belief that your bond is unbreakable, and that the connection transcends physical distance.

## Release attachment to the 3D manifestation

![twin flame separation, divine feminine healing, twin flame sex, twin flame union](/images/2023/06/twin-flame-separation-7.jpg)

Let go of expectations regarding the physical aspects of the relationship. Understand that the twin flame journey is about soul growth and spiritual evolution. Shift your focus from the external circumstances and trust in the divine timing of your reunion. Surrender to the higher plan and embrace the journey, even if it unfolds differently from what you initially envisioned.

That doesn't mean patiently waiting. It doesn't mean that once you so this, you will come into union. That is an attachment to an outcome. Try to take comfort in the fact that you are as deeply loved as they are, and that in itself is a blessing - as painful as it may be. many people never experience this kind of love, and it is truly magical to share your soul with someone so deeply, even if it is only temporary. There is a reason for your journey, but release the expectation of twin flame union.

## Nurture your own happiness from within

![twin flame separation, divine feminine healing, twin flame sex, twin flame union](/images/2023/06/twin-flame-separation-2.jpg)

Recognize that you are responsible for your own happiness and well-being. Instead of relying solely on the presence of your twin flame for fulfillment, give yourself everything you need to be happy from within. Cultivate self-love, practice gratitude, and embrace joy in your own life. Explore your passions, cultivate meaningful connections with others, and create a fulfilling life that nourishes your soul.

Even if that means dating other people in the meantime, you may have energies to work out in your relations with other people. Welcome the people that come into your life in the interim, and just remember that even if it doesn't compare to the twin flame love you share, it still has value and meaning.

## Seek support and guidance

![twin flame separation, divine feminine healing, twin flame sex, twin flame union](/images/2023/06/twin-flame-separation-4.jpg)

One of the most comforting things during the twin flame journey is to share your experience with others. A twin flame connection can be very confusing and overwhelming, and it can be extremely helpful to share your experience with others who can relate. Most of us are familiar with the feeling of being misunderstood when we talk about our twin flames. People chalk it up to being toxic because they don't understand.

Remember that a true twin flame connection is never abusive. The pain of dealing with the obstacles are never intentional. It is simply a result of each partner needing to do their own work on their own timeline. Everyone's journey is different.

So, seek support and guidance from people who have experienced the twin flame journey. Surround yourself with a supportive network of friends, family, or spiritual communities who can offer understanding and guidance. Seek out mentors, coaches, or therapists who have experience with twin flame connections and can provide insight and support during this challenging period. Remember, you don't have to go through this journey alone.

## Surrender and practice patience

![twin flame separation, divine feminine healing, twin flame sex, twin flame union](/images/2023/06/twin-flame-separation-6.jpg)

Embrace the art of surrender and practice patience. Trust that the universe is orchestrating your twin flame journey in the most divine and perfect way. Allow the process to unfold naturally, without trying to force or control the outcome. Surrendering allows for personal growth, healing, and alignment with the higher wisdom of the universe.

Remember, twin flame separations are an integral part of the journey towards union and self-realization. Embrace the lessons and growth opportunities that come with this phase, and have faith that the connection you share with your twin flame is eternal. Stay true to yourself, nurture your own well-being, and trust that love will guide you back together in divine timing.

## Setting psychic boundaries - you are in control

During a twin flame separation, it is essential to set psychic boundaries if you require space and solitude. Remember, you are in control of your psychic space, and no one can enter without your permission. Take the time to honor your own needs and prioritize your well-being. Visualize a protective energetic shield around you, envisioning it as a loving barrier that allows in only positive and nourishing energies. Practice grounding techniques, such as deep breathing or connecting with nature, to reinforce your energetic boundaries. Trust yourself and assert your boundaries with confidence, knowing that taking care of your own energetic space is an act of self-love and self-preservation. By doing so, you create a safe and sacred space for your own healing and personal growth during this transformative period of twin flame separation.

## Meditate on Unconditional Love

Meditating on unconditional love can be a powerful practice to maintain a high vibrational state during a twin flame separation. By centering your attention on the boundless and limitless nature of love, you anchor yourself in a state of positivity and compassion. During meditation, visualize a radiant sphere of love surrounding you and your twin flame, transcending any physical distance or temporary separation. Allow this love to flow freely, enveloping both of you in its warmth and healing energy. As you connect with the energy of unconditional love, it uplifts your spirit, brings peace to your heart, and helps you stay aligned with the higher purpose of your twin flame journey. Through this practice, you cultivate a vibration of love and understanding, paving the way for a harmonious reunion and the deepening of your sacred connection.

## When it is happening then it is the right time

Remember that when the twin flame union happens, then it is the right time, not the other way around. It's not about fixing ourselves so that we can achieve twin flame union - that is a form of attachment. Also, try to avoid analyzing what your twin needs to overcome or accomplish to make your twin flame union possible. The true lesson of a twin flame separation is to accept and surrender to the fact that separation is an illusion. Your love is real, no matter how your 3D lives are manifesting.
